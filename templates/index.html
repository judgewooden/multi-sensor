Welcome to JPH Monitor

I have multiple computers on one water cooling loop. The water travels through my walls to a cupboard outside where a radiator get rid of the heat. This is a monitoring tool to provide a dashboard on the status of the system.

The component diagram shows all the components I use to monitor the system.

  • The Arduino monitors the flow sensors and has a fail-safe feature to automatically detect a leak and switch of the power to avoid the pumps from running dry when I am not home.
  • The pi-kast monitors the temperature of the radiator and ambient temperature.
  • The pi-host monitors the water temperature on the manifold and power meters.
  • The pi-monitor monitors the flow and run the JPH monitoring software.
